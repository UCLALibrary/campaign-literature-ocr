CAMPAIGNS, POLITICAL - 1996 -

Retiring Congressman Tony Beilenson
strongly supports Democrat Brad Sherman
to succeed him in Congress.

¢ Brad Sherman will fight to protect federal
clean air and water standards and to stop
Republican plans to sell off the Santa Monica
Mountains to developers.

¢ Brad Sherman will vigorously oppose efforts
to slash Medicare and Social Security
benefits.

¢ As a member of the State Board of
Equalization, Brad Sherman helped stop a
billion dollar tax grab by foreign-owned multi-
national corporations.

¢ As a member of the Board of California
Common Cause, Brad Sherman worked for
campaign and ethics reform and to lessen the
influence of big money special interests.

BRAD SHERMAN

United States Congress
