Proposing to extend permanently the Section 127 exclusion for employer-provided
educational assistance. The section, which expired in 1994, allowed employees to exclude
employer-provided educational assistance from their taxable income. In addition, the
President has proposed allowing small businesses a 10 percent tax credit for amounts paid for
employee education and training under a Section 127 program.
